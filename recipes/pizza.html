Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for the well-known Italian dish called pizza

Although there are many variations, we chose one that is liked by many

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Pizza dough
  • Mozarella
  • Pepperoni
  • Basil
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Knead dough flat into a circle shape
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the flattened dough
  4. Add mozzarella
  5. Add pepperoni
  6. Add basil leaves
  7. Cook in the oven
