Hamburger

Description

This is a recipe for the well-known dish called hamburger

Although there are many variations, we chose one that is liked by many

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Beef patty
  • Onions
  • Hamburger buns
  • Mayonnaise
  1. Lightly toast and butter the hamburger
  2. Grill the beef patty
  3. Assemble with all the ingredients in the preferred order
